El Gran Combo de Puerto Rico's short BIO:

El Gran Combo de Puerto Rico, commonly known as El Gran Combo, is a salsa group from Puerto Rico. It is one of the most recognized salsa groupd in Latin America. Celebrating its 50th anniversary in 2012, it is considered Puerto Rico's most successful musical group. The group received the moniker La Universidad de la Salsa (The University of Salsa) in Colombia, due to the sheer number of famous salsa musicians and singers who developed their careers with it, who started with the group (particularly Andy Monta√±ez), or who were occasionally backed up by the band and La India.
